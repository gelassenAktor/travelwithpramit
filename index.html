5 Best Places to visit in Berlin

  1. Brandenburger Tor

    The Brandenburger Tor in Berlin is an early classicist triumphal gate that stands on the western flank of the square Pariser Platz in the Mitte district of Berlin.

  2. Reichtagsgebäude

    The Reichstagsgebäude on the Platz der Republik in Berlin has been the seat of the German Bundestag since 1999. The Federal Assembly has also met here since 1994 to elect the German Federal President.
